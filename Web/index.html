Our Research

Our team is proposing to develop OrchiZen, an intelligent orchid cultivation system that enhances the accuracy and efficiency of farming practices by integrating Machine Learning, Internet of Things (IoT), and automation technologies. The system addresses critical challenges in irrigation, species selection, fertilization, and disease management through real-time data analysis and AI-powered decision-making.

Main components of our research :
  1. 1. Automated Mist Irrigation System for optimal and species-specific watering schedules.
  2. 2. Orchid Species Recommendation Engine using environmental data and Artificial Neural Networks.
  3. 3. AI-based Fertilizer Recommendation System using YOLOv11 and Decision Trees for plant growth stages.
  4. 4. UV-C Based Disease Detection and Grow Light Optimization using AI and IoT integration.

Our Research

Introduction

🌸 OrchiZen – Smart Orchid Farming

In today’s evolving agricultural landscape, precision and sustainability are essential for maximizing yield and minimizing resource waste. Traditional orchid farming methods often lead to inconsistent care, low productivity, and high operational costs due to manual irrigation, generic fertilization, and delayed disease detection.

Our team developed OrchiZen, an AI-powered smart farming system that enhances the efficiency and accuracy of orchid cultivation. By integrating machine learning, IoT, and automation, it delivers intelligent solutions for mist irrigation, species selection, fertilizer recommendation, and UV-based disease management—transforming traditional farming into data-driven precision agriculture.

Abstraction

Traditional orchid farming in Sri Lanka faces critical inefficiencies in irrigation, species selection, fertilization, and disease management due to reliance on manual methods and generic practices. This research introduces OrchiZen, an AI-powered smart farming system that enhances the accuracy and efficiency of orchid cultivation through machine learning, IoT, and automation. The system integrates real-time environmental sensing and AI models to automate microclimatic mist irrigation, recommend orchid species based on location, provide image-based fertilizer planning, and enable early fungal detection with UV-C treatment. By streamlining cultivation processes and reducing human error, OrchiZen delivers a scalable solution that significantly increases productivity, minimizes resource usage, and supports sustainable orchid farming. Future enhancements will focus on fertigation integration, crop expansion, market-demand forecasting, and adaptive learning for broader agricultural impact.

Main Objective (Overall System)

Develop an intelligent orchid cultivation system to enhance the accuracy and efficiency of farming practices by integrating machine learning, IoT, and automation technologies. The system automates critical processes such as irrigation scheduling, species selection, fertilizer recommendation, and disease control—ensuring optimized plant care, reduced resource waste, and increased crop productivity.

Main Objective (Automated Mist Irrigation)

Develop an automated microclimatic mist irrigation system that uses real-time sensor data and rule-based logic to deliver optimal hydration for orchid plants, reducing water waste and promoting healthy growth.

Sub Objectives (Automated Mist Irrigation)

Collect real-time data such as humidity, temperature, and light using IoT sensors (DHT11, LDR).

Analyze environmental parameters to determine optimal misting frequency and duration.

Control misting mechanism via ESP32 microcontroller for precise irrigation.

Simulate rainforest-like conditions to enhance orchid hydration and growth.

Main Objective (Species Recommendation)

Develop an AI-powered orchid species recommendation system that analyzes localized climate conditions using artificial neural networks (ANN) to suggest the most suitable orchid type for a given environment.

Sub Objectives (Species Recommendation)

Continuously collect environmental data (temperature, light, humidity) from the plantation site.

Feed median sensor values into the ANN model to remove anomalies.

Predict the optimal orchid species for the microclimatic condition (Dendrobium, Phalaenopsis, Vanda).

Improve yield by ensuring climate compatibility and optimal growth conditions.

Main Objective (Fertilizer Recommendation)

Develop an AI-based fertilizer recommendation system that uses YOLOv11 object detection and decision tree algorithms to assess plant features and provide stage-specific nutrition plans for orchid growth optimization.

Sub Objectives (Fertilizer Recommendation)

Capture plant images (leaves, stems, roots) through the mobile interface.

Use YOLOv11 to detect plant parts and determine age and condition.

Classify growth stage using a Decision Tree and recommend proper nutrients.

Prevent over- or under-fertilization by applying targeted nutrition plans.

Main Objective (UV Disease Control & Light Optimization)

Develop a UV-C based disease management system with AI-driven fungal detection, integrated with an automatic grow light adjustment mechanism to maintain optimal lighting conditions and enable chemical-free disease prevention.

Sub Objectives (UV & Light Optimization)

Analyze leaf images using CNN to detect early-stage fungal symptoms.

Trigger UV-C light treatment to eliminate pathogens without chemicals.

Monitor ambient light using LDR sensors and adjust grow light via AC dimmer.

Ensure species-specific lighting conditions to support optimal flowering and energy efficiency.
